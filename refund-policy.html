Cancellation & Refund Policy

Clear timelines and conditions for cancellations and refunds.

Last updated: June 2025

Refund requests are processed within 5–7 business days of approval. Please read the conditions below before submitting a request.

1. Free Trial

InstiQ offers a free trial period upon registration. No payment is required during the trial. The trial automatically ends on the expiry date, after which a paid subscription is required to continue using the Service. No refund is applicable for the free trial period as no charge is made.

2. Subscription Cancellation

  • You may cancel your InstiQ subscription at any time from within the app or by contacting support@instiq.in.
  • Upon cancellation, your access to the Service will continue until the end of the current billing period.
  • No further charges will be made after cancellation takes effect.
  • Cancellation does not automatically trigger a refund for the current billing period — see Section 3 for refund eligibility.

3. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are considered under the following conditions:

  • Within 7 days of first payment: If you are a first-time paying subscriber and raise a refund request within 7 days of your initial payment, you are eligible for a full refund.
  • Service outage or technical failure: If InstiQ experiences a service outage lasting more than 48 consecutive hours and you are unable to use the platform, a pro-rata refund may be issued for the affected period.
  • Duplicate payment: If a duplicate charge was processed due to a technical error, a full refund will be issued for the duplicate amount.

4. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued in the following situations:

  • Requests made after 7 days of the billing date (except in cases of service outage or duplicate payment)
  • Partial use of the subscription period
  • Change of mind after the free trial has already been used
  • Accounts suspended due to violation of our Terms & Conditions
  • Downgrade from a higher plan to a lower plan mid-cycle

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team at support@instiq.in with the following information:

  • Your registered email address
  • Institute name
  • Payment date and transaction reference (if available)
  • Reason for refund request

Our team will review your request and respond within 3 business days. If approved, the refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

6. Plan Upgrades and Downgrades

  • Upgrades: When upgrading to a higher plan, a pro-rata charge is calculated for the remaining days in the current billing cycle. The new plan takes effect immediately.
  • Downgrades: When downgrading to a lower plan, the change takes effect at the start of the next billing cycle. No refund is issued for the difference in the current cycle.

7. Contact

For any questions about this policy, please reach out to us at support@instiq.in or visit our Contact page.
