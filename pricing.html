Frequently asked questions

What happens after the free trial?

After 14 days, your account is paused. You can choose a plan and continue with full access — no data is lost during the transition.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately with a pro-rata charge. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

Is there an annual discount?

Annual pricing options are coming soon. Contact us at support@instiq.in to discuss options for your institute.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit and debit cards, UPI, and net banking through our secure payment provider.

Can I get a refund?

Yes — see our Cancellation & Refund Policy for full details. First-time subscribers can request a refund within 7 days of their first payment.
